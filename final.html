Welcome to The Generic Modder!

The Generic Modder is the place to download mods for your favorite games!
If this is your first time visiting this website, create an account here! If you already have an account, login here!
We strive to be a friendly community of people who make mods, and help others make mods for
our favorite games. Use the forum to collaborate on mods with other people, or upload your own mods and get feedback!

Mod Making

If you are a modder and would like to create and submit your own mods, follow these steps:

  • Create an account using the link above.
  • Create a mod for your game. If you dont have access to any modding tools, use this tool
  • Submit your mod here
  • After that, keep an eye on the forum for feedback!

If you get stuck or confused, head over to the forum where you can engage with the community and get help!
